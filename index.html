About Me

A Little About Me

Hi, I’m Varun T.
I’m a UI/UX Designer who enjoys making designs that look good and are easy to use. I have experience in wireframing, interaction design, usability testing, and prototyping with Figma.

I have completed internships at Nandha Infotech, Zidio Development, Tresveze & Co, and Trapigo Technologies, where I also worked on live projects. These experiences helped me understand how to turn ideas into real designs that people can use. I have also worked on personal projects to practice and improve my skills.

I’m always excited to learn new things, explore creative ideas, and work on projects that create simple and useful experiences for people.
